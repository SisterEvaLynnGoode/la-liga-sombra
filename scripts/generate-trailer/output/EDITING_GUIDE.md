## Part 5 — CapCut Assembly Walkthrough (Chromebook)

**Step 1 — New project**
1. Open [capcut.com](https://www.capcut.com) in Chrome
2. Click **New Project** → set ratio to **9:16**
3. Upload all 9 MP4 files from `scripts/generate-trailer/output/`

**Step 2 — Arrange clips**
1. Drag Shot 1 to the timeline first
2. Add Shots 2–9 in order
3. Trim Shot 6 to ~4 seconds (cut after the tear appears)
4. Trim Shots 7–8 to 3s and 1.5s respectively
5. Keep Shot 9 at ~1.5 seconds — the abrupt cut to black is intentional

**Step 3 — Add transitions**
- Shot 1→2: "Dissolve" (0.5s)
- Shot 2→3: Hard cut (no transition — the title card needs to hit)
- Shot 5→6: "Dissolve" (0.5s) — emotional beat, let it breathe
- All other cuts: Hard cut (no transition — pace builds)
- Shot 9 ending: Add "Fade to Black" on the last clip (0.5s)

**Step 4 — Add music**
1. Click **Audio** → **Upload** → import your chosen track
2. Trim to 35 seconds
3. Set volume to -8dB
4. Duck under VO: use "Volume" keyframes, drop to -18dB at each VO line

**Step 5 — Add VO**
1. If using ElevenLabs: export each line, import separately
2. If recording yourself: Click **Audio** → **Record**
3. Place each audio clip at the timestamps in Part 2

**Step 6 — Add text overlays** (see Part 1 table)
1. Move playhead to 0:10 → Click **Text** → **Add Text**
2. Type `LA LIGA SOMBRA`
3. Choose a bold brush-style font (search "brush" in the font picker)
4. Color: mustard yellow (#C9933A)
5. Add entrance animation: "Typewriter" or "Brush In"
6. Repeat for `Un misterio en español` at 0:13 in red (#C0392B)
7. At 0:33.5: Add `JUSTICIA` in large bold red with "Stroke" or "Ink" animation
8. At 0:34: Add `¿Puedes atraparlos?` in white, smaller, below JUSTICIA

**Step 7 — Add SFX** (optional but powerful)
1. Import freesound clips as separate audio track
2. Place at timestamps from Part 4
3. Set volume to -12dB (they support, not overpower)

**Step 8 — Color grade** (optional)
1. Select all clips → **Adjust** panel
2. Saturation: -20 (deepen the noir feel)
3. Contrast: +15
4. Temperature: -5 (slightly cooler)

**Step 9 — Export**
- Resolution: 1080×1920 (CapCut will upscale from 720p — acceptable)
- Frame rate: 24fps
- Quality: High

## Part 7 — Accessibility Checklist

- [ ] **Burned-in English subtitles** on the Spanish VO (add as text layer in CapCut, white with black outline, bottom of frame)
- [ ] **No strobe effects** — all cuts are clean; electrical sparks in Shot 7 are brief (< 3 flashes/second ✅)
- [ ] **High contrast text** — mustard on black and red on black both pass WCAG AA at large sizes
- [ ] **Captions file** — export a .srt from the VO script and attach to YouTube/Instagram upload
- [ ] **Audio description** available as a separate audio track (optional — describe key visual action)
